Bin Features

Encircling view of the custom bin subsystem used on the IEEE competition robot.

  • Custom switches determine retracted versus extended states.
  • Lead screw is motor-driven to carry the pusher plate between endstop switch points.
  • Servo simultaneously expands the ball retention space.
  • Servo mechanism also holds the bins so the robot can move them around the game field.
